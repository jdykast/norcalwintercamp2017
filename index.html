NorCal Winter Camp

January 27-29, 2017

In His Image

“So God created man in His own image; in the image of God He created him.”
Genesis 1:27

From the very beginning mankind was created after a particular image for a special purpose. But all around us these truths are under attack. This year we’re dedicating winter camp to reminding ourselves of these things and their application to our lives.

Join us for a weekend of powerful Bible teaching, passionate worship, and meaningful fellowship. Join us for a weekend set apart to God!
